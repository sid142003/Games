How to Play

Welcome to Hotel Manager Dash! Here's how you can manage your hotel and maximize your income:

  • Add Guests: Click the 'Add Guest' button to add a new guest. If all rooms are occupied, and you have enough income, a new room will be created automatically.
  • Drag and Drop: Drag guests to any available room. Remember, guests prefer the room type they've chosen (normal or upgraded).
  • Upgrade Rooms: Click on a normal room to upgrade it for a fee. Upgraded rooms generate more income.
  • Income and Expansion: Each guest increases your income. Use your income wisely to upgrade rooms and expand your hotel by adding new rooms when necessary.
  • Timer: You have 2 minutes in each round to earn as much as you can. The game offers 3 rounds, and your highest income will be recorded.
  • Goal: Try to maximize your income and the number of rooms during the given time. Can you become the ultimate hotel magnate?
